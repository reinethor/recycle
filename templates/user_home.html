Glass

There are three basic types of glass: flint, amber and emerald. No matter what type of glass it is, before it’s recycled, it must be broken down into smaller pieces. For this reason, you shouldn’t place mixed colors of glass into your recycling bin. Once it’s mixed up, there’s no easy method of organization again.

{% endif %} {% if info.day is equalto 8 %}

How much waste is actually recycled?

Out of all of our waste America only recycles about a third of it. This might not sound too bad, but Mother Nature Network puts it into perspective for us.

“That's not bad, considering less than 7 percent of the country's trash was recycled in 1960, and nearly 94 percent of it wound up in landfills. But since that 94 percent totaled only 82.5 million tons, and today's 54 percent totals 132 million tons, it's clear the job is far from finished.”
